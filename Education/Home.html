Persistent, structured engagement spaces purpose-built for institutional use — far more controllable than informal group chats, and fully owned and moderated by the organization.

  • Member management with role-based permissions
  • Admin, moderator, and member role levels
  • Topic-based channels for focused discussion
  • Announcements for official communications
  • Threaded posts and discussions
  • Embedded live sessions within communities
  • Private, public, or invite-only access settings
  • Fully searchable, moderated content

Speed Networking Engine

Facilitates real, face-to-face interaction between members, alumni, and professionals through automated matching and timed rotations — with minimal moderation burden on staff.

  • Automated random or criteria-based participant matching
  • Timed conversation rotations between participants
  • Moderator controls and full session oversight
  • Scalable from small groups to large events

User Profiles & Expert Directory

Every participant has a rich, searchable profile — turning the platform into a living, browsable directory of expertise, availability, and services that members can self-navigate without any coordinator.

  • Professional bio, photo & expertise tags
  • Published service listings & live availability calendar
  • Session history and optional ratings & reviews
  • Social and portfolio links (LinkedIn, personal site)
  • Verification badges issued by administrators
  • Filterable by topic, specialty, or keyword
